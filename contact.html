Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a logo? +
Basic logo designs are delivered within 24-48 hours. Premium packages with multiple concepts may take 2-3 days. Rush delivery is available for urgent projects.
What is the website development process? +
The process includes: 1) Discovery call to understand requirements, 2) Design mockup for approval, 3) Development with clean code, 4) Testing and revisions, 5) Launch and deployment. Typical timeline is 3-7 days depending on complexity.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes! All packages include revisions. Basic: 1-2 revisions, Business: 3 revisions, Premium/AI: Unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I accept payments via Bank Transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, and International transfers through Wise/Payoneer. 50% advance to start the project.
Can you work with international clients? +
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. All communication is done via WhatsApp, Email, or Zoom. Time zone differences are never an issue.
